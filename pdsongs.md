Notes
-----

-   Project Mirai 2 includes all the songs from Project Mirai 1.
-   Project Mirai Deluxe is an updated version of Project Mirai 2.
-   Future Tone is the name given to the update of Project Diva Arcade that introduced the slide bar. For purposes here, "Arcade" will refer to the arcade and "Future Tone" will refer to the home port on the PS4.
-   Titles should follow "Casing of Titles" according to the Chicago Manual of Style. Exceptions are allowed and should be tagged with \[sic\].
    -   ALL-UPPERCASE TITLES
    -   all-lowercase titles
    -   Title Case, All-Uppercase, and All-Lowercase can change inside/after parentheses, brackets, or dashes.
-   Songs are sorted alphabetically by title.
-   In the case of multiple titles, official titles found in games take precedent
-   In the case of multiple official titles, the title that comes first alphabetically is the one considered for sorting
-   Specific version of the song should be listed
    -   Unless multiple versions across all of the Diva/Mirai series exist, in which case only the "root title" is listed.

Annotations
-----------

-    A indicates song available in AR Mode only (Vita)
-    D indicates song available as DLC
-    E indicates song in an Extra (free DLC)
-    M indicates song included partially in a medley (DX)
